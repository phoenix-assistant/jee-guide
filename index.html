JIIT Noida — Jaypee Institute of Information Technology

PRIVATE DEEMED JEE MAIN DIRECT NAAC A++ · Noida Sector 62 & 128 · Est. 2001

✅ No separate exam. No board % cutoff. Direct admission on JEE Main rank.

JIIT doesn't publish closing ranks like JoSAA/JAC. They admit broadly based on JEE percentile + merit ranking. A student at 38K (96.5 percentile) gets guaranteed admission to all branches and likely qualifies for scholarship (10–25% tuition waiver).

  • Official cutoff portals (JAC Delhi, JoSAA, IPU) use JavaScript-rendered dynamic pages that blocked programmatic scraping

  • All closing ranks are approximate ±2,000–3,000 ranks

  • IPU data uses CET ranks, not JEE ranks
